Focus protocol recommended

  • Deep work: 45–50 min blocks, one subject, phone away.
  • Breaks: 10 min after each block; 25–30 min after three blocks.
  • Review: End each day with 15 min recall (no new content).
  • Sleep: Protect 7.5–8 h nightly — it beats extra midnight hours.
